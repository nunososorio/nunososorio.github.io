Augmented Reality in Education: Unveiling the Present and Shaping the Future of Active Learning

This two-hour session was designed specifically for teachers interested in exploring the transformative potential of augmented reality (AR) and related technologies in education. We will examine the existing technologies in this domain and discuss their potential impact on teaching. We will present practical examples of AR applications in various scientific areas and how they can be developed, inside and outside the classroom, using different platforms. We will also discuss the future trends of AR technologies and their potential to shape the future of active learning.

Thursday . 19 October 2023

Innovative Teaching with App-Free and Web-Based Augmented Reality

Augmented Reality (AR) is a technology that superimposes digital information on a physical object or environment, enhancing how it's perceived. It has gained importance in education because of its ability to engage students' curiosity and interest. AR can be used to create dynamic and interactive learning experiences by adding digital elements while maintaining the interaction between students, teachers, and the real world. Cost and accessibility of AR devices and software has decreased significantly, and many students today carry advanced mobile devices with built-in AR functionality. This workshop will introduce participants to the possibilities of AR in education and share ideas for integrating it into classroom practice. Participants will learn how AR can be used to create dynamic and interactive learning experiences, as well as suggestions for methodological integration in an educational context.

Our Vision

The xR-EDUM has organically emerged from the need to share ideas, methodologies, and overcome challenges related to the application of technologies known as xR in teaching. xR is an umbrella term for Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Mixed Reality (MR). The members of this community recognize the revolutionary potential these technologies can have in promoting successful learning. They share the avant-garde vision of using xR effectively in an educational context, complementing active teaching approaches and promoting meaningful learning for their students.

Digital technologies will continue to revolutionize the way we live and learn. In particular, xR technologies are currently being used in top schools worldwide to enhance student learning. The requirements for these approaches range from a simple smartphone or tablet to VR/AR glasses with integrated haptic accessories. These new technologies empower teaching and learning methodologies and offer vast opportunities to expand reality and increase simultaneous interaction between people, real objects, and digital ones.

About Us

xR-EDUM is a vibrant community that unites educators from different areas in a shared mission: to revolutionize learning through the power of Extended Reality (xR).


The members of this community include:

Whether it's Augmented Reality (AR), Mixed Reality (MR), or Virtual Reality (VR), we're harnessing these cutting-edge technologies to create immersive, interactive educational experiences that captivate students and ignite their curiosity.

Though our roots are at the University of Minho in Portugal, our community is open. We welcome teachers of all levels, from any institution or country, who share our passion for xR and its potential to transform education.
